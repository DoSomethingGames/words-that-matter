Words That Matter

A DoSomething.org game about healthy relationships

You're in high school and are getting ready for prom with your date. When given a choice, click the blue choice buttons to navigate your way through the evening.

Designed and programmed by Jasmine Mithani, Andrea Gonzales, and Jonathan Uy.
